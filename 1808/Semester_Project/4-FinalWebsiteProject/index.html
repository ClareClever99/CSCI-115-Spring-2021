Introduction

Artificial intelligence, also known as AI, is an area of study in the field of computer science. AI is concerned with the development of computers able to engage in human-like thought processes such as learning, reasoning, and self-correlation.

AI has been used in several different lines of work ranging from a hospital room to the battlefield.

Artificial intelligence is changing the world. It is used almost everywhere and has limitless possibilities. AI was developed to simplify the mundane tasks that humans perform.

AI has come a long way since it began to become mainstream in the 1950s. From the very beginning, AI’s goal was to be useful to humans.

However, numerous questions regarding the usefulness of AI still exist… Has AI gone too far? Has it reached the point of no return? It is all too hard to tell.

In this ever-emerging technical world, AI has become a necessary tool used by many Fortune 500 companies. Those who do not use artificial intelligence will be left behind.

The good thing is that AI is an umbrella term for many different forms of algorithms used to make computers act intelligently.

All companies could benefit from AI. Determining how AI will be used and maintained will lead to a better understanding of the future and its ethical landscape.
