Future of AI

The impact of AI is seen everywhere. AI is here to stay. Lots of things are being simplified with the help of artificial intelligence. AI will continue to learn and improve, as well as repeatedly be better than the day before. Artificial intelligence used to be a technique only employed by top researchers and scholars.

In today’s modern world, almost anyone can use AI libraries and tools. With the ever-increasing use of AI, its foundations must be taught to the masses. Ethical classes will build the foundation upon which programmers can learn to construct better, more ethically robust AI machines. The future of AI is not in AI itself but within computer programmers’ control.

Conclusions

Leading AI to an ethical future will be difficult, but not impossible. Fostering better ethical awareness in the field of artificial intelligence will help it become the great technological innovation originally envisioned. AI is programmed by humans, to help humans. Calling AI unethical is the same as calling its programmers unethical.

Ethical AI must start at the hands of the programmers. Allowing for a better ethical foundation on which AI algorithms are built will prove immensely beneficial to the future of AI. The future of AI is in the world’s hands. How this weighty responsibility will be handled has yet to be seen or fully realized.
